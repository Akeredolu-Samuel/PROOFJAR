# 🏺 ProofJar: The AI-Powered Accountability Protocol
**Solana Hackathon Submission**

## 💡 The Problem
Self-improvement is a $10B industry, yet 92% of New Year's resolutions fail. The primary reason? **Lack of immediate consequence.** Social accountability (telling a friend) is weak because friends are too kind. Financial accountability (betting money) is hard to automate and trust.

## 🚀 The Solution
**ProofJar** is a decentralized accountability protocol that uses **AI Agents** to verify real-world progress and **Solana Smart Contracts** to enforce financial commitments.

### The "Commit-Verify-Earn" Loop:
1.  **Commit**: Users lock SOL into a non-custodial vault for a specific goal (e.g., "Hit the gym 5 days this week").
2.  **Verify**: Users upload proof (images, videos, or text).
3.  **Audit**: A **Gemini-powered AI Agent** analyzes the proof against the commitment. No human intervention needed.
4.  **Reward**: If verified, the SOL is returned (or rewards are earned). If failed, the "Jar" is slashed or distributed to charity/stakers.

---

## ⚡ Technical Innovation (The "Dev3" Pack)

### 1. AI-in-the-Loop Verification
ProofJar moves beyond simple timestamp checks. By integrating AI agents directly into the verification pipeline, we can audit complex human behaviors (e.g., "Did this photo actually show a finished workout?") without centralized gatekeepers.

### 2. High-Performance Solana Vaults
Utilizing Solana's sub-second finality and low fees, ProofJar creates instant micro-escrows. Each challenge is its own program-derived address (PDA), ensuring users always maintain custody of their funds until the challenge conditions are met.

### 3. Premium UX
Privacy and complexity are hidden behind a "DeFi-for-Humans" interface.
*   **Plena Wallet Integration**: Seamless onboarding for non-crypto natives.
*   **Mobile-First Design**: Built for the "on-the-go" nature of accountability.
*   **Framer Motion Orchestration**: Premium micro-interactions that make hitting goals feel like a game.
